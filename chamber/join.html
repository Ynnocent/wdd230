Membership Options

NP Membership

The NP Membership is our free membership option that allows you to stay informed about our events, news and updates. You will receive our monthly newsletter and be invited to attend our networking events.

Bronze Membership

Our Bronze Membership offers additional benefits to enhance your business growth. You will receive discounts on our training programs and events, as well as have the opportunity to showcase your business on our website and social media platforms.

Silver Membership

Our Silver Membership provides you with even more opportunities to promote your business and connect with other local businesses. You will receive priority access to our networking events and be able to participate in our mentorship program.

Gold Membership

Our Gold Membership is our premium membership option that provides exclusive benefits to help you grow your business. You will have access to all of our training programs and events, as well as receive personalized business coaching and consulting services. Additionally, you will have the opportunity to join our board of directors and help shape the direction of the Chamber.

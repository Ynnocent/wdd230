Carlsbad

Carlsbad is a coastal city located in the North County region of San Diego County, California. It has a population of approximately 115,000 people and is known for its beautiful beaches, resorts, and world-renowned golf courses. Some of the popular attractions in Carlsbad include LEGOLAND California, The Flower Fields at Carlsbad Ranch, and the Carlsbad Premium Outlets. The city is also home to several technology companies and is known for its thriving business community.

About Us

Bountiful Foods was founded in 1965 by John and Mary Smith, who started the company in their small kitchen. They began by selling homemade jams and jellies at local farmers' markets, and their products quickly became popular due to their delicious taste and high quality.

Over the years, Bountiful Foods expanded their product line to include other gourmet food items, such as artisanal cheeses, cured meats, and baked goods. They opened their first retail store in 1970, and their business continued to grow as more and more people discovered their delicious products.

In the 1980s, Bountiful Foods began to focus on sustainability and organic farming practices, becoming one of the first companies in the industry to do so. They also started to source their ingredients locally, supporting small farmers and promoting community-based agriculture.

Today, Bountiful Foods is a thriving company with a strong commitment to quality, sustainability, and community. Their products can be found in gourmet food stores and restaurants across the country, and they continue to innovate and explore new flavors and food trends.

Target Audience

The target audiences that this website is trying to aim, are Bank investors U.S. Department of Treasury, as well as other businesses in the town of the Shire.

Investment Banker

Name: Cilbo Caggins

Job Title: Bank Investor

Age: 49

Gender: Male

User Motivation: Cilbo Caggins's main goal is to find new investment opportunities and to network with other business leaders in the community. He is likely to be interested in attending events and networking opportunities hosted by the Shire Chamber of Commerce, as well as looking for information on local businesses and entrepreneurs that may be suitable for investments. He is also not tech-savy.

Government Official

Name: Caladriel Von Dutch

Job Title: Government Administrator

Age: 55

Gender: Female

User Motivation: Caladriel's main goal is to stay informed about local businesses development and potential funding opportunities in the Shire's local businesses and organizaitons. She is likely to be interested in attending events and networking opportunities hosted by the Shire Chamber of Commerce, as well as looking for information on local businesses and organizations that maybe eligible for government funding. She adores simplistic design.
